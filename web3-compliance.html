THE CHALLENGE

The Fundamental Challenge in Web3 Compliance

Traditional compliance systems were designed for centralized financial institutions where customer identities are known and transactions flow through regulated channels. However, blockchain introduces a paradigm shift:

Pseudonymity

Blockchain addresses don't inherently reveal real-world identities

Decentralization

Transactions occur without intermediaries

Global Access

Anyone can participate without geographic restrictions

Immutability

Once executed, transactions cannot be reversed

This creates a significant gap between traditional compliance frameworks (designed for named entities) and blockchain transactions (based on pseudonymous addresses).
